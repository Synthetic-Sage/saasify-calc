Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

SaaSifyCalc is a completely static utility website. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not store the financial data or metrics you input into our calculators. All calculations are performed instantly in your browser (locally on your device).

2. Cookies and Advertising

We use third-party advertising companies (such as Google AdSense) to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

3. Log Files

Like most standard web site servers, we use log files. This includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at our GitHub issues page.
