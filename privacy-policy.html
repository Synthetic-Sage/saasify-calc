Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

We respect your privacy. This application is built with a privacy-first, local-first architecture. All calculations, data entry, and file generations occur directly within your web browser on your local device. We do not transmit, collect, or store any of your sensitive personal data on our servers.

2. Local Storage and Cookies

The application may use your browser's Local Storage (localStorage) to save your preferences or in-progress work so you don't lose data when refreshing the page. This data remains on your device. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or invasive analytics.

3. Third-Party Links

Our tools may contain links to external sites that are not operated by us. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites.

4. Security

Because your data is not transmitted to us, the security of your data relies on the security of your own device and browser. We recommend keeping your operating system and web browser up to date.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and will be effective immediately upon posting.
